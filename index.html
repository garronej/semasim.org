Welcome to Semasim.org

Semasim is an application that enables you to

  • Use multiple Sim cards with your mobile phone
  • Make phone calls and send text messages from anywhere in the world as if you were at home to avoid roaming fees
  • Share your sim card(s) with other users so that they can answer on your behalf

Semasim implements end to end encryption which will guarentee the privacy of your phone calls and text messages.

This website provides step by step instructions on how to install a Semasim server on a Raspberry pi, Orange pi, MAC or PC.

All the information you need about the required hardware is provided in the documentation
